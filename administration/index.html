Platform Administration

Tealium provides a unified permissions system to manage user access to your account and profiles through permission groups and admin roles.

How to tell which version of permissions your account is using:

  • Legacy permissions - The admin menu displays Manage Users. To migrate your account from legacy to platform permissions, contact your Customer Success Manager.
  • Platform permissions - The admin menu displays Manage Permissions.
    • If the Platform Permissions screen displays Permissions Enforcement, you are using the EEA version of platform permissions.

The GA version of platform permissions is identical to the EEA version except for the presence of Permissions Enforcement.

If you are still unsure which version of permissions your account uses, contact your account administrator.
