Partner Integrations

Learn how to integrate your platform with the Tealium Customer Data Hub.

This partner integration guide is for you if you answer “Yes” to any of these:

  • Are you a marketing technology vendor that wants to integrate with the Tealium Customer Data Hub?
  • Do you want your JavaScript tag offered in the Tealium Tag Marketplace to allow your customers a seamless setup process in Tealium iQ Tag Management?
  • Do you have a server-to-server API and want your integration offered in the Tealium Connector Marketplace to provide your customers with a seamless setup process in Tealium EventStream API Hub or Tealium AudienceStream CDP?
  • Do you have a mobile SDK and want to offer a native integration with Tealium for Mobile?

The Tealium Customer Data Hub is designed to support a variety of inbound and outbound data integrations. Become a partner and integrate your technology.

For more information, select one of the integration types below:

Data Sources

Learn how to send data into Tealium from your application using inbound data integrations.

CMP Plugins

Learn how to integrate your Consent Management Platform (CMP) with Tealium iQ Tag Management to enforce privacy settings for managed tags.

CMS Plugins

Learn how to integrate the Tealium Universal Tag (utag.js) with your content management system.

Tags

Learn how to integrate your browser-based tag with Tealium iQ Tag Management.

Mobile

Learn how to integrate your mobile SDK with Tealium for Mobile.

Connectors

Learn how to integrate your server-side API for real-time activation of event and customer data in Tealium.
