---
title: Undeploy a model
description: This article describes how to undeploy one or more versions of a trained model.
url: https://docs.tealium.com/predict/deploy/undeploy-a-model/
---
There are two ways to undeploy your model:

1. From the **Model Dashboard**, click **Undeploy**.  
      ![](/images/predict/predictv2-undeploy.png)
1. From the **Training Details** screen, after reviewing the training details you can choose to undeploy.  
      ![](/images/predict/predictv2-training-details-undeploy.png)

After you select to undeploy your model, complete the following steps:

1. Click **Undeploy**.  
Your model is now _Version 2_ with a status of _Requires Publish to Undeploy_.
1. Save and Publish your changes.  
After you save and publish, this status of this version of your model returns to _Trained_.
