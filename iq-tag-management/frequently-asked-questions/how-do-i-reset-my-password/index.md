## Question

How do I reset my password?

## Answer

To reset your password, you must have access to your email account. Make certain that `noreply@tealium.com` is not blocked in your email client.

Use the following steps to reset your password:

1. From the [login screen](https://my.tealiumiq.com), click **Forgot Password?**
1. Enter your email address, then click **Submit**.
1. The Tealium platform will send a **Reset Password** email with instructions to the provided email address. Log in to that email account.
1. Follow the instructions in the email. Click the **Reset My Password** link to reset your password.
1. Enter your new password in both fields. Make certain your new password meets the minimum requirements displayed on the screen.

Tealium does not send passwords through email, so if you forgot your password, you must reset it.

## Additional Information

If you know your password, but need to troubleshoot MFA, see [Multi-Factor Authentication]().

These steps do not apply if you use single sign-on (SSO). See [Using Single-Sign On (SSO) with Tealium]().
