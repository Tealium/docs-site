## Action Settings - Parameters and Options

Click **Next** or go to the **Actions** tab. This is where you configure connector actions.

This section describes how to set up parameters and options for each action.

### Action - Send Conversion

#### Parameters

| **Parameter**          | **Description**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
|:-----------------------|:--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Kayak Click ID         | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;(Required) The KAYAK unique identifier for the click/lead sent to your site.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;This value is case-sensitive.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| Booking ID             | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;(Required) Your unique identifier for the booking.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;This should be the unique identifier of the booking and used in all subsequent reports.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| Kayak Commission       | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;(Required) The commission KAYAK earned on the booking, in the currency specified by the Commission Currency parameter.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Do not include currency symbols, such as `$`.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Limit to 6 decimals.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| Commission Currency    | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;(Required) Currency of Kayak Commission.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;This should be the currency in which commission to KAYAK is paid, if possible.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Use the [ISO 4217](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_4217) codes.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| Conversion Time        | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;(Optional) The date time of when the booking was first made, in your report&#39;s timezone.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Use the `YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ssTZD` format, an [ISO 8601 simplification by W3C](https://www.w3.org/TR/NOTE-datetime).&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;If not provided, the current timestamp will be used.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                     |
| Price                  | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;The gross booking value of the booking, in the currency specified by the currency parameter.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Do not include currency symbols, such as `$`.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Limit to 6 decimals&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| Currency               | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;Currency of price.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Use the [ISO 4217](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_4217) codes.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;This parameter is required whenever price parameter is used.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| Booking Type           | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;Type of booking.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Must be one of `flight`, `hotel`, `car`, `package`, `cruise`, `train`, `restaurant`, or `activity`.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| Commission Percentage  | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;Commission rate applied to this booking to calculate the commission.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;This should be a decimal number between `0` and `1`, and limited to 6 decimals.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| Tag 1                  | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;Custom field for additional data.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| Tag 2                  | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;Custom field for additional data.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| Check In Date          | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;The check in date of the booking.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Use the `yyyy-mm-dd` format.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Use the local time zone for the property.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| Check Out Date         | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;The check out date of the booking.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Use the `yyyy-mm-dd` format.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Use the property&#39;s local time zone.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| HID                    | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;Your unique identifier for the property booked, which is the same as provided in the data feed to us and used in the API.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| Number of Travelers    | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;Total number of travelers (all ages).&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| Number of Rooms        | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;The number of rooms booked.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| Flight Origin          | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;[IATA code](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IATA_airport_code) for the initial origin airport in the booked itinerary.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| Flight Destination     | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;[IATA code](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IATA_airport_code) for the destination airport of the first leg of the booked itinerary.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| Fare Class             | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;Comma-separated list of fare basis codes for each segment in booking.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Must be ordered by departure date.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;If all flight segments share the same fare basis code, a single value can be used.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Map Array type attributes to add multiple fare classes.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Map single value attribute to add a single fare class.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                |
| Cabin Type             | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;Comma-separated list of cabin class booked for each segment in booking.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Possible values are `First`, `Business`, `Premium Economy`, or `Economy`.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Must be ordered by departure date.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;If all flight segments share the same cabin class, a single value can be used.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Map Array type attributes to add multiple cabin types.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Map single value attribute to add a single cabin type.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt; |
| Leg Index              | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;Zero-indexed leg number.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| Segment Index          | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;Zero-indexed segment number.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| Departure Airport Code | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;Departure Airport Code&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| Arrival Airport Code   | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;Arrival Airport Code&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| Departure Date Time    | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;Use the `yyyy-mm-dd` format.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| Arrival Date Time      | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;Use the `yyyy-mm-dd` format.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| Carrier Code           | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;[IATA code](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IATA_airport_code)&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| Flight Number          | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;Flight Number.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Map Array type attributes to add multiple flight itineraries.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Array type attributes must be of equal length.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Map single value attributes to add a single flight itinerary.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| Car Pick-up Date       | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;The date of car pick-up, in the local time zone of the pick-up location.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Use the `yyyy-mm-dd` format.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| Car Drop-off Date      | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;The date of car drop-off, in the local time zone off the drop-off location.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Use the `yyyy-mm-dd` format.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| Car Pick-up ID         | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;Your unique identifier for the pick-up location for this booking, which is the same as provided in the data feed to us and used in the API.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| Car Drop-off ID        | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;Your unique identifier for the drop-off location for this booking, which is the same as provided in the data feed to us and used in the API.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| Car Agency             | &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;Your unique identifier for the car agency for this booking, which is the same as provided in the data feed to us and used in the API.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
