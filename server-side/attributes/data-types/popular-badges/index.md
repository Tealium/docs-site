---
title: Popular Badges
description: These are popular Badges that have proven useful to other clients.
url: https://docs.tealium.com/server-side/attributes/data-types/popular-badges/
---
| Badge | Description |
|:-----:| ----------- |
| ![](/images/server-side/whiteui-badge-browseabandoner.png)&lt;br&gt; Browse Abandoner |  &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Rule** – A visitor who has viewed a product and did not add an item to the cart.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Action** – If a Known Visitor and Facebook ID is known, send data to Facebook to retarget the brand to the visitor.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt; |
| ![](/images/server-side/whiteui-badges)business-family.png)&lt;br&gt; Business/Family Traveler |  &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Rule** – Assign when the visitor marks their travel type.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt; |
| ![](/images/server-side/whiteui-badges-cartabandoner.png)&lt;br&gt; Cart Abandoner |  &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Rule** – A visitor who has added an item to the cart and did not purchase.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Action** – If email address is known, send a personalized email with the abandoned items.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt; |
| ![](/images/server-side/whiteui-badges-couponcodeabuser.png)&lt;br&gt; Coupon Code Abuser |  &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Rule** – A visitor attempts to use more than a certain number of coupon or promotion codes in a single visit.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt; |
| ![](/images/server-side/whiteui-bades-couponcodestuffer.png)&lt;br&gt; Coupon Code Stuffer |  &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Rule** – A visitor has successfully used more than a certain number of coupon or promotion codes across all visits.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt; |
| ![](/images/server-side/whiteui-badges-disappointedshopper.png)&lt;br&gt; Disappointed Shopper |  &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Rule** – Assign when a visitor views a product that is currently out of stock.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt; |
| ![](/images/server-side/whiteui-badges-freetrialuser.png)&lt;br&gt; Free Trial User |  &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Rule** – Assign when a visitor has signed up for a Free Trial.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt; |
| ![](/images/server-side/whiteui-badges-frequenttraveler.png)&lt;br&gt; Frequent Traveler |  &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Rule** – Assign when the visitor has traveled more than a certain number of times over a given time period, for example: Visitor has traveled 5 times in the last 12 weeks.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt; |
| ![](/images/server-side/whiteui-badges-knownvisitor.png)&lt;br&gt; Known Visitor |  &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Rule** – Assigned when we capture an important identifier of the visitor, for example: Customer ID (client&#39;s ID), Customer Email, Facebook ID. These visitors commonly targeted through email or social media.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Action** – None, but used in an Audience to ensure the visitor is targetable with a selected vendor API.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt; |
| ![](/images/server-side/whiteui-badges-loyalvisitor.png)&lt;br&gt; Loyal Visitor |  &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Rule** – Assign when a visitor has a certain number of direct visits to the site.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt; |
| ![](/images/server-side/whiteui-badges-newvisitor.png)&lt;br&gt; New Visitor |  &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Rule** – Assign when &#34;Lifetime visit count&#34; equals 1.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt; |
| ![](/images/server-side/whiteui-badges-purchaser.png)&lt;br&gt; Purchaser |  &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Rule** – A visitor who has converted, for example: purchased a product or signed up for a service.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Action** – Email in 2 weeks with an offer enticing to make another purchase or an email showing related items for purchase, for example if the visitor bought a baseball bat offer a baseball bag and batting gloves.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Action** – Remove visitor from retargeting campaign so they are not remarketed unnecessarily.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt; |
| ![](/images/server-side/whiteui-badges-recentvipvisitor.png)&lt;br&gt; Recent VIP Visitor |  &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Rule** – Assign when their rolling 90-day purchase value is over a certain dollar amount.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt; |
| ![](/images/server-side/whiteui-badges-salesearcher.png)&lt;br&gt; Sale Searcher |  &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Rule** – Assigned when a visitor views more than a certain number of sale pages, no matter whether is the sales category or a product on sale.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt; |
| ![](/images/server-side/whiteui-badge-browseabandoner.png)&lt;br&gt; Search Abandoner |  &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Rule** – A visitor who has landed on the site from a search engine and has not shown completed a common action, such as viewing at least 3 pages in the visit.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Action** – Determine the category searched and retarget with a Display Ad vendor.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt; |
| ![](/images/server-side/whiteui-badges-siteresearcher.png)&lt;br&gt; Site Researcher |  &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Rule** – Assign when a visitor has more than a certain number of visits to the site and the average visit duration is over a specific number of minutes.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt; |
| ![](/images/server-side/whiteui-badges-testuser.png)&lt;br&gt; Test User |  &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Rule** – Assigned when &#34;test&#34; is in the URL, or debug cookie is set, or some other criteria that indicates a test visitor.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;Allows an Audience and Action to be tested in production before a campaign goes live to ensure setup is correct.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt; |
| ![](/images/server-side/whiteui-badges-unknownvisitor.png)&lt;br&gt; Unknown Visitor |  &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Rule** – Assigned when the visitor has yet to authenticate on the website. Authenticate does not necessarily mean they have logged in, if a visitor comes to the site from an email and the email is in the URL then we&#39;ve determined who the visitor is and they have &#34;authenticated&#34;,&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Action** – Display a modal to the visitor encouraging them to identify themselves. These visitors are more commonly targeted through Display Ad.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt; |
| ![](/images/server-side/whiteui-badges-recentvipvisitor.png)&lt;br&gt; VIP Visitor |  &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Rule** – Assign when lifetime purchase value has increased to a certain dollar amount.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt; |
| ![](/images/server-side/whiteui-badges-windowshopper.png)&lt;br&gt; Window Shopper |  &lt;ul&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Rule** – A visitor who visits the site often but does not purchase, for example: a visitor who has been to the site 3 times in the last 7 days, has viewed a product details page or read an article, and has not purchased or signed up for a service.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;li&gt;**Action** – Determine their affinity to a category and change the landing page of the next session to personalize the experience.&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt; |
