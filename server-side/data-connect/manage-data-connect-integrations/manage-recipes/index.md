## View recipes and recipe details

The **Integrations** screen displays available assets, which are recipes and app connections. If you have a large number of recipes and app connections, you can create folders to organize them.

Complete following steps to filter, search, and view details of a recipe:

1. Navigate to **Connect > Data Connect > Integrations**.
1. To exclude connections from the list, click **Recipes**.
1. To filter the list by recipe status, click **All statuses**, and select **Active** or **Inactive**.
1. To search for a recipe, click the **Search** box and enter part of the recipe name.  
The list is filtered as you enter the name.
1. To see details for a specific recipe, click the recipe.

## Edit a recipe

Complete following steps to edit a recipe:

1. Navigate to **Connect > Data Connect > Integrations**.
1. Click **Recipes**, then click on a recipe.
1. Click **Edit recipe**.
1. Modify the trigger and actions as needed.
1. To test the recipe, click **Test**.
1. When your changes are completed, click **Save**.

## Start or stop a recipe

You can stop an active recipe or start an inactive recipe. Complete following steps to start or stop a recipe:

1. Navigate to **Connect > Data Connect > Integrations**.
1. To search for a recipe, click the **Search** box and enter part of the recipe name.  
The list is filtered as you enter the name.
1. Click the recipe menu and select **Start** or **Stop**.
