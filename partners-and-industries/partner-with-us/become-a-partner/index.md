This partner integration guide is for you if you answer "Yes" to any of these:

* Are you a marketing technology vendor that wants to integrate with the Tealium Customer Data Hub?
* Do you want your JavaScript tag offered in the Tealium tag marketplace to allow your customers a seamless setup process in Tealium iQ Tag Management?
* Do you have a server-to-server API and want your integration offered in the Tealium Connector Marketplace to provide your customers with a seamless setup process in Tealium EventStream API Hub or Tealium AudienceStream CDP?
* Do you have a mobile SDK and want to offer a native integration with Tealium for Mobile?

The Tealium Customer Data Hub is designed to support a variety of inbound and outbound data integrations. [Become a partner](https://tealium.com/technology-partners/) and integrate your technology.

For more information, select one of the integration types below.

## Integration types

* **[Data Sources](https://docs.tealium.com/data-sources/)** - Learn how to send data into Tealium from your application using inbound data integrations.
* **[Tags](https://docs.tealium.com/tags/)** - Learn how to integrate your browser-based tag with Tealium iQ Tag Management.
* **[CMP Plugins](https://docs.tealium.com/cmp-plugins/)** - Learn how to integrate your Consent Management Platform (CMP) with Tealium iQ Tag Management to enforce privacy settings for managed tags.
* **[CMS Plugins](https://docs.tealium.com/cms-plugins/)** - Learn how to integrate the Tealium Universal Tag (utag.js) with your content management system.
* **[Mobile](https://docs.tealium.com/mobile/)** - Learn how to integrate your mobile SDK with Tealium for Mobile.
* **[Connectors](https://docs.tealium.com/connectors/)** - Learn how to integrate your server-side API for real-time activation of event and customer data in Tealium.
